Running for Charity and how you can set up your own fundraiser

Running for Charity is a brilliant way to not only challenge yourself and beat a personal best, but also raise money and awareness to a cause close to your heart. Not only can you push yourself knowing it is for a great cause it couldn't be more simple to get those donations in by setting up a go fund me page. The three simple steps are:

  • Sign up to a Marathon ☑️
  • Set up a Go Fund Me Page ☑️
  • Share to social media, friends and family ☑️

Ready to set yourself a new challenge? Check out a Marathon or half Marathon near you

The TSC London Marathon takes place each year in April. It is the largest annual fundraisng event since it began in 1981 where participants have raised more than £1 billon for amazing causes. Just think you could be adding this humungous amount. To find out more visit the TCS London Marathon Run for charity page here

Setting up your GoFundMe

Setting up a GoFundMe page to raise money for charity couldn't be easier, GoFundMe is a custom built platform that allows you to set goals, tell your story of why you are running for a charity, add pictures/videos and best of all it is free! With may built in ways to share your page across all social media platforms you will be raking in the donations in no time. GoFundMe advise the best way to keep donations flowing is updating them on journey. You can include updates like training for the marathon, how much you have raised already and include content about the amazing cause you have selected. To start your page and find out more about GoFundMe visit their webpage by clicking the image. Good Luck!
